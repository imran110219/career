# Senior Java Developer

## Role context

InScale is recruiting for Teletrac Navman, a SaaS fleet-management provider that uses location-based technology to help customers manage vehicles, equipment, and light assets. The product integrates real-time data from IoT-enabled devices and is used globally.

As a senior member of the R&D team, the successful candidate will help shape and enhance a large-scale fleet-management platform. The role covers the full software-development lifecycle: concept and design, development, testing, release, and continuous improvement. It offers scope to guide developers and influence technical direction while collaborating with distributed, cross-functional teams.

## Responsibilities

- Develop, enhance, and maintain Teletrac software solutions.
- Guide application developers and technical leads in creating and owning technical products through sound process and technology choices.
- Take technical responsibility across specification, implementation, validation, release, maintenance, and continual enhancement, ensuring adherence to development standards and documented requirements.
- Maintain and improve business-support and process tools as needed.
- Analyse requirements for new products, working directly with end users and product management.
- Drive a customer-focused delivery and execution culture.
- Maintain proficiency across a broad range of technology products and platforms.

## Technical requirements

### Must have

- 7+ years of Java software-development experience.
- Experience using AI-assisted development tools.
- Database design and performance-optimization experience.

### Good to have

- AWS or other cloud technologies in a DevOps environment.
- Test-automation frameworks and continuous integration.
- Agile development experience.
- Designing and implementing microservices architectures.
- Experience with a message queue or streaming platform, such as RabbitMQ, SQS, Kafka, or Kinesis.
- Location services, GIS, or geospatial-industry knowledge.

## Collaboration and leadership expectations

- Solve complex problems and communicate practical, creative solutions clearly.
- Work effectively independently and in a team; improve software quality and share knowledge.
- Show technical and team-leadership interest, including mentoring developers at different experience levels.
- Take ownership, remain accountable, and work constructively with diverse stakeholders.
- Use automation and innovation to help the business operate and grow.
- Communicate effectively in writing and verbally at all organizational levels.

## Education

Degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Information Systems, Software Engineering, or an equivalent technical discipline and/or equivalent experience.
