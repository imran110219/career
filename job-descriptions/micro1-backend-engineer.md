# Backend Engineer

## Job Summary

Apply backend expertise to help train next-generation AI systems through high-quality, real-world input. No prior AI experience is required; domain knowledge is the focus.

## Key Responsibilities

- Design, develop, and maintain scalable backend services and APIs that meet demanding performance requirements.
- Collaborate cross-functionally to translate business needs into technical specifications and impactful backend solutions.
- Optimize backend systems for reliability, security, and efficiency, ensuring seamless user experiences.
- Write clean, well-documented, and thoroughly tested code, adhering to engineering best practices.
- Participate actively in code reviews, mentoring, and knowledge-sharing sessions.
- Monitor, troubleshoot, and resolve production backend issues, targeting root causes and continuous improvement.
- Contribute to architecture discussions and decisions with a solutions-oriented mindset.

## Required Skills and Qualifications

- Backend development expertise using modern programming languages.
- Experience designing and implementing RESTful APIs and microservices architectures.
- Relational and NoSQL database proficiency, including data-modeling principles.
- System security, authentication, and authorization best practices.
- Cloud-platform experience (AWS, GCP, or Azure) and containerization (Docker, Kubernetes).
- Strong written and verbal communication, collaboration, and proactive working style.
- Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced, fully remote expert-team environment.

## Preferred Qualifications

- Familiarity with event-driven or serverless architectures.
- CI/CD pipelines and automated testing frameworks.
- Mentoring or technical leadership in backend teams.
