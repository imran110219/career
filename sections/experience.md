# Experience

Use this file to maintain canonical experience entries before tailoring them for individual resumes.

## Entry Template

```markdown
### Company Name - Role Title

Location or Remote | Month YYYY - Month YYYY

- Scope: Team size, product area, systems owned, users served, or business domain.
- Impact: Most important measurable result.
- Technical depth: Stack, architecture, data, infrastructure, or operational detail.
- Leadership: Mentoring, planning, review, hiring, cross-functional work, or stakeholder communication.
```

## Bullet Quality Checklist

- [ ] Starts with a strong action verb.
- [ ] Explains personal contribution.
- [ ] Includes relevant technical detail.
- [ ] Shows business, reliability, performance, cost, or team impact.
- [ ] Avoids vague claims that cannot be defended in an interview.

## Action Verbs

| Theme | Verbs |
| --- | --- |
| Build | Built, implemented, developed, shipped, automated |
| Improve | Optimized, reduced, simplified, stabilized, refactored |
| Lead | Led, coordinated, mentored, guided, reviewed |
| Design | Designed, architected, modeled, specified, documented |
| Operate | Monitored, diagnosed, resolved, hardened, supported |
