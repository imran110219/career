# Lead Backend Mock Interviews

## 60-Minute Lead Backend Mock

| Time | Segment |
| --- | --- |
| 5 min | Current role and leadership scope. |
| 15 min | Delivery scenario. |
| 15 min | Technical design and trade-off discussion. |
| 15 min | STAR story: conflict, incident, mentoring or risk communication. |
| 10 min | Candidate questions and compensation/logistics if appropriate. |

## Scenario Set

- Lead delivery of a sensitive workflow with payment, identity and reporting dependencies.
- Improve code-review quality without slowing urgent delivery.
- Recover a delayed backend feature while keeping stakeholders aligned.
- Resolve disagreement between modular monolith and microservice approaches.

## Compensation Preparation

Keep compensation notes public-safe. Do not commit private salary limits or offer details.

- Research market range before recruiter screens.
- Ask for the approved compensation range for the level.
- Evaluate total compensation, not only base salary.
- Confirm work model, relocation, timezone and visa-sponsorship expectations.
