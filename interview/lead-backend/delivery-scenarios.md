# Delivery Scenarios

Practise answers that show hands-on leadership and technical delivery.

## Scenario Prompts

- A product owner gives an ambiguous workflow requirement two weeks before a deadline. How do you clarify and break down the work?
- A frontend team depends on an API that is still changing. How do you manage the contract?
- A developer proposes a microservice for a feature that could fit the existing service. How do you decide?
- A pull request solves the happy path but misses authorization and failure handling. How do you respond?
- A release is at risk because database migration scope changed late. What do you do?
- A production incident involves multiple services and unclear ownership. How do you lead the response?
- A stakeholder wants speed, while the team is worried about quality. How do you communicate risk?
- A delayed project needs recovery without hiding technical debt. What is the plan?

## Answer Checklist

- Clarify business outcome and user impact.
- Identify dependencies, risks and unknowns.
- Split work into deliverable increments.
- State quality gates: tests, review, security, observability, rollback.
- Separate personal actions from team actions.
- Close with result, follow-up and evidence status.
