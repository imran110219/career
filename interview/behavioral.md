# Behavioral Interview Notes

Use structured stories that show judgment, ownership, collaboration, and learning.

## Story Bank

| Theme | Story | Outcome |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Technical leadership | Add example of leading a complex delivery effort. | Add measurable outcome. |
| Conflict | Add example of resolving disagreement constructively. | Add decision and result. |
| Failure | Add example of missed expectation or incident. | Add learning and process change. |
| Mentorship | Add example of helping another engineer grow. | Add resulting ownership or performance. |
| Ambiguity | Add example of turning unclear requirements into a plan. | Add delivery or alignment outcome. |

## STAR Structure

| Section | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| Situation | Brief context and stakes. |
| Task | Your responsibility. |
| Action | Specific decisions and actions you took. |
| Result | Measurable outcome and what changed afterward. |

## Checklist

- [ ] Prepare at least six stories.
- [ ] Include leadership, conflict, failure, mentorship, and ambiguity.
- [ ] Keep each answer under three minutes.
- [ ] Quantify outcomes where possible.
- [ ] Practice follow-up questions for each story.
