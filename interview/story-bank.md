# Interview Story Bank

Use these prompts to prepare factual STAR stories. Replace every bracketed item with a verified detail; do not invent an outcome.

## Production incident

**Candidate context:** Election-platform distributed integrations or deployment troubleshooting.  
Situation: [service/workflow and user impact] · Task: [your exact responsibility] · Action: [diagnosis, coordination, fix] · Result: [verified result or NEEDS REVIEW].

## Slow database query

**Candidate context:** Exam-system Redis and backend performance work.  
Situation: [slow workflow] · Task: [ownership] · Action: [baseline, caching/query/design change] · Result: reported 30% response-time reduction; explain measurement method if verified.

## Ambiguous requirements

**Candidate context:** Candidate review, nomination, or exam-management requirements.  
Situation: [ambiguity] · Task: [clarify scope] · Action: [model, API contract, acceptance criteria] · Result: [verified delivery outcome].

## Architecture disagreement

Situation: [decision and alternatives] · Task: [your role] · Action: [tradeoffs, evidence, alignment] · Result: [decision and follow-up].

## Mentoring

Situation: [engineer/team need] · Task: [support boundary] · Action: [pairing, design review, code review] · Result: [verified growth or delivery outcome; otherwise NEEDS REVIEW].

## Access-control issue

**Candidate context:** Keycloak-backed role controls and validation.  
Situation: [unwanted-data-change risk] · Task: [security responsibility] · Action: [authorization/validation changes] · Result: [verified risk reduction; otherwise state controls added, not a numeric outcome].

## Distributed integration issue

**Candidate context:** SMS, payment, identity-verification, master-data, or reporting integration.  
Situation: [failure chain] · Task: [coordination/technical role] · Action: [contract, logging, retry, validation] · Result: [verified outcome].

## High-pressure delivery

**Candidate context:** Election-period traffic or result-workflow digitization.  
Situation: [deadline/stakes] · Task: [delivery scope] · Action: [planning, task allocation, deployment support] · Result: [reported scale only when approved].

## Failed deployment

**Candidate context:** One of approximately 30 deployment contributions.  
Situation: [failure] · Task: [your role] · Action: [Dockerfile/diagnosis/collaboration] · Result: [verified recovery and prevention].

## Quality-versus-speed tradeoff

Situation: [deadline] · Task: [decision owner] · Action: [minimum safe scope, validation, rollout plan] · Result: [quality/delivery evidence].
