# Salary Negotiation Notes

Use this file to prepare compensation discussions before recruiter screens and offer calls.

## Compensation Targets

| Item | Target |
| --- | --- |
| Base salary | Research and define before interviews. |
| Bonus | Confirm structure, target percentage, and payout history. |
| Equity | Understand vesting, refreshers, strike price, and liquidity. |
| Benefits | Health, retirement, leave, learning budget, equipment. |
| Work model | Remote, hybrid, relocation, travel, timezone expectations. |

## Negotiation Principles

- Anchor on market data, role scope, and demonstrated impact.
- Avoid sharing a current salary unless legally required and strategically useful.
- Evaluate total compensation, not only base salary.
- Ask for written details before making final decisions.
- Keep tone collaborative and specific.

## Questions To Ask

- What is the approved compensation range for this level?
- How is level determined and calibrated?
- What are the bonus and equity refresh practices?
- How often are compensation reviews conducted?
- What flexibility exists across base, bonus, equity, sign-on, and remote support?

## Checklist

- [ ] Define acceptable, target, and stretch compensation numbers.
- [ ] Research market data for role, level, location, and company stage.
- [ ] Identify non-compensation priorities.
- [ ] Prepare a concise negotiation script.
- [ ] Review offer details in writing before accepting.
