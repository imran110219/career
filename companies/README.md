# Company Application Workspace

Use this area to research companies, prepare targeted application packages, and track application status.

## Recommended Company Folder

```text
companies/applications/company-name/
├── README.md
├── job-description.md
├── resume-notes.md
├── cover-letter.md
├── recruiter-message.md
└── interview-notes.md
```

| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Add company | Add role | Remote or city | Job board, referral, recruiter | YYYY-MM-DD | Researching | Identify hiring manager |

## Research Checklist

- [ ] Product, customers, and business model are understood.
- [ ] Role responsibilities are mapped to resume evidence.
- [ ] Required and preferred skills are separated.
- [ ] Interview process and likely evaluation areas are noted.
- [ ] Compensation range, location, and work authorization constraints are clear.
- [ ] Potential concerns or tradeoffs are documented.
